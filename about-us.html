About us

Fresh, Pure, and Wholesome Dairy Products

Golden Yellow delivers the finest of dairy products that stand for purity, excellence, and unmatched quality. Founded in 2024 under the helm of

Vallhabha Milk Products Pvt. Ltd (Estd 2018)., Golden Yellow symbolizes premium dairy craftsmanship at its best. Being the pride of the legendary Chittoor Cow Belt from Andhra Pradesh, our products express the richness and nutritional value of that region.


Golden Yellow offers a select range of high-quality dairy products- curd, butter, paneer, and malai paneer-all crafted with unique quality. Our focus lies in balancing traditional values with modern innovation to bring you the best of dairy products for body and soul nourishment.


Rooted in a legacy of trust and sustainability, Golden Yellow embodies the commitment of Vallhabha Milk to quality and customer satisfaction. Advanced production facilities and strict quality checks ensure that every product you enjoy is fresh, wholesome, and full of natural goodness.


Join us on a journey to experience the pure, creamy indulgence of Golden Yellow—where tradition meets innovation, and every bite is a celebration of taste and quality.

Our Mission
