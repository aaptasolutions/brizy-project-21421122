Leadership Team

Home >> Leadership Team

At Golden Yellow, our success is driven by an experienced and visionary leadership team dedicated to excellence in the dairy industry. With decades of expertise and a shared commitment to quality, they guide our brand toward sustainable growth and innovation.

Bolla Brahma Naidu - Chairman

A veteran in the dairy industry with over 30 years of experience, Mr. Naidu co-founded Tirumala Milk in 1994, successfully scaling it into a market leader before its acquisition by Lactalis Group for INR 1,750 Crore. His strategic vision and leadership continue to inspire the growth and success of Golden Yellow under Vallhabha Milk Products Pvt. Ltd.

Edara Nageswara Rao Director

With over 40 years of experience and a PGDM from IIM Ahmedabad, Mr. Rao has held leadership roles in prominent dairy companies, including Heritage, Godrej, Jersey, and Tirumala. His vast expertise drives our company’s strategic expansion and operational efficiency.

Bolla Giri Babu
Managing Director

An experienced leader with a Master’s degree from one of Australia's leading institutions, Mr. Giri Babu brings over a decade of hands-on experience in the dairy sector. His passion and dedication have been instrumental in the brand’s growth and operational success.

R. Srinivas
Director

A qualified Dairy Technologist with 28 years of industry experience, Mr. Srinivas specializes in procurement, production, and marketing. Having held senior roles at companies like Heritage and Tirumala, his contributions drive excellence across operations.

V. Subbaiah
Director

With over 20 years of expertise in accounting and finance, Mr. Subbaiah was a key figure at Tirumala Milk. His proficiency in financial planning and operations ensures Golden Yellow maintains a strong and sustainable financial foundation.

Ch. Giri Babu
Director

A skilled engineer with over 18 years of experience, Mr. Giri Babu’s focus on procurement and production management enhances efficiency, ensuring consistent quality in our premium dairy products.

V. Saidaiah
CEO

A qualified Chartered Accountant, Mr. Saidaiah has been with the company since 2019. As CEO, he leads overall strategy, accountability, and business growth, ensuring Golden Yellow delivers on its promise of quality and customer satisfaction.
